TurboWarp Privacy Policy

Updated September 6th, 2020.

What information is collected

When the use the TurboWarp website (turbowarp.org), some information may be automatically logged. These logs can include:

  • IP addresses
  • User agents
  • Referring sites
  • Site usage information
  • Timestamps

Some of this information, such as IP addresses, may be personally identifiable.

When TurboWarp connects to the TurboWarp cloud variable server, the following additional information is logged:

  • Project IDs
  • Usernames chosen by the user (not necessarily your real Scratch username)
  • Other generic usage information
  • In case of errors, some additional information may also be logged

The cloud variable server automatically purges all variable data of a project within 24 hours of no one being connected to that project. This data cannot be recovered.

How this information is used

  • To provide the service
  • To prevent abuse

Data Retention

Logs created by TurboWarp are kept for up to 14 days, unless compelled by law to keep it longer.

Third Parties

This privacy policy does not apply to third parties. Notable third parties include:

Cloudflare

Cloudflare is used to accelerate and protect the TurboWarp website. Read the Cloudflare privacy policy.

jsDelivr CDN

The Scratch 3 Dev Tools plugin is loaded from the jsDelivr CDN. Read the jsDelivr CDN privacy policy.

Scratch

Projects may be loaded from the Scratch website. Read the Scratch privacy policy.

Sentry

Sentry may be used to monitor errors experienced by users. Read the Sentry privacy policy.

Contact

Any concerns related to privacy or any other matter should be sent to: contact@turbowarp.org
