TurboWarp Privacy Policy

Updated October 11th, 2020.

What information is collected

  • IP addresses
  • User agents
  • Referring sites
  • Site usage information
  • Project IDs (when connecting to cloud variable server)
  • Usernames (when connecting to cloud variable server)
  • Timestamps

Some of this information, such as IP addresses, may be personally identifiable.

How this information is used

  • To provide the service
  • To prevent abuse
  • To monitor use of the service

How this information is shared

  • Personally identifiable information is never shared, unless necessary to provide the service or prevent abuse
  • Anonymous aggregated statistics, such as number of users per day, may be shared

Data Retention

  • Personally identifiable information is deleted within 14 days
  • Aggregated statistics may be kept longer

Third Parties

This privacy policy does not apply to third parties. A non exhaustive list of notable third parties include:

Scratch

Projects may be loaded from the Scratch website. Read the Scratch privacy policy.

jsDelivr CDN

The Scratch 3 Dev Tools are loaded from the jsDelivr CDN. Read the jsDelivr CDN privacy policy.

Contact

Any concerns related to privacy or any other matter should be sent to: contact@turbowarp.org
