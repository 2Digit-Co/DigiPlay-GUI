TurboWarp Privacy Policy

Updated August 15th, 2020.

What information is collected

When the use the TurboWarp website (turbowarp.xyz), some information may be logged through automated means such as webserver logs. These logs can include IPs, user agents, referring websites, and site usage information. Some of this information may be personally identifiable, such as IP addresses.

When TurboWarp connects to the TurboWarp cloud variable server, the following information is also logged:

  • IP addresses (usually partially anonymized by truncating bytes)
  • Usernames chosen by the user (not necessarily your real Scratch username)
  • Project IDs
  • User agents (Operating system, device information, etc.)
  • Timestamps
  • Other generic usage information
  • In case of errors, some additional information may also be logged

How this information is used

  • To provide the service
  • To prevent abuse

Data Retention

Up to 14 days, unless compelled by law or something like that to keep it longer.

Third Parties

This privacy policy does not apply to third parties. Notable third parties include:

Cloudflare

Requests to the TurboWarp website may be proxied through the Cloudflare network. Read the Cloudflare privacy policy.

jsDelivr CDN

The Scratch 3 Dev Tools plugin is loaded from the jsDelivr CDN. Read the jsDelivr CDN privacy policy.

Scratch

Projects may be loaded from the Scratch website. Read the Scratch privacy policy.
